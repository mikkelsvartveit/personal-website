Speedcubing

Speedcubing is all about solving the Rubik's cube as quickly as possible. I first learnt how to solve the cube in 2014, and today I can do it in around 10 seconds. I am ranked top 10 in Norway in both the classic 3x3 cube, the smaller 2x2 variant as well as in one-handed solving.
